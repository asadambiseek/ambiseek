Program Fees

We offer Training and Internship programs across Europe of 1-month and 2-months duration. The program fees varies according to the City you would be travelling to and the duration you would choose.

Note: Program fees do not include visa fees, flights, Insurance or daily food & drink.

The Program Includes :

Professional Internship
Accommodation
24/7 Support
Airport Pickup
Visa Assistance
Social Events
Career Training
Network of International Contacts

Application Process

1. Apply

  • No application fee.
  • Schedule an interview by phone or google meet with us.
  • Evaluation of your interview and resume/CV by us

2.Enroll

  • If you are accepted into the program, congratulations!
  • Confirm your place in the program by making a deposit of 50% of your fee.

3. Remaining balance due

  • You will be asked to pay the remaining 50% balance within 21 days of having your internship role confirmed.

4. Pack your bags

  • You are ready to start the adventure of a lifetime!
