Application Process

1. Apply

  • No application fee.
  • Schedule an interview by phone or google meet with us.
  • Evaluation of your interview and resume/CV by us

2.Enroll

  • If you are accepted into the program, congratulations!
  • Confirm your spot by making a deposit of INR 25,000.

3. Half of balance due

  • 50% of your remaining fee is due within 15 days of enrollment.

4. Remaining balance due

  • You will be asked to pay the remaining balance within 30 days of your enrollment.

5. Pack your bags

  • You are ready to start the adventure of a lifetime!
