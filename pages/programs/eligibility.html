Eligibility

We provide programs for candidates at all stages of their academic or professional career, from gap year applicants to experienced professionals looking to change careers. In order to obtain a visa for most of our programs, candidates need to be 18 or above.

We will guide you through the process of getting the necessary documents to intern in Europe.

  • Passport valid for a minimum of six months with three photocopies of the personal information and photo page
  • 4 recent pictures, passport size
  • Completed study visa application form of the European country you will be travelling to
  • Proof of admission to the AmbiSeek program as a full-time student (provided by us)
  • Private health insurance policy
  • Original medical certificate typed on doctor stationery verifying that the applicant is free from the following quarantine diseases: yellow fever, cholera, and the plague. The certificate must also certify that the applicant is free of drug addictions and mental illness. A translation into relevant language must be provided
  • Confirmation that accommodation details while in Europe have been paid for in full (provided by us)
  • Proof of financial means for the period of study in Europe. This could be: Certificate of enrollment stating educational and living expenses have been paid for in advance, OR
  • Notarized letter of parents/custodians, assuming financial responsibility while student is in Europe, or current statement of the studentâ€™s bank account with a minimum balance of 80,000 INR per month of stay in Europe or 80,000 INR worth of travelers checks per month of stay in Europe.
  • Note: requirements change depending which consulate you are going to.
