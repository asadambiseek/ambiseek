Founders

Asad Alam

Founder & Director

Asad Alam is the Founder & Director of AmbiSeek. He is also the CEO at Ajab Technologies Pvt. Ltd. which is one of the fastest growing tech company in Dubai and India since 2014. He acquired his B.Tech Degree in Computer Science from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology in Pune, India followed by Master’s Degree in International Business from IESEG School of Management in Paris, France.

Akshay Jadhav

Founder & CEO

Akshay Jadhav is the Founder & CEO of AmbiSeek. Prior to founding AmbiSeek he practiced Civil and Criminal Law at Mumbai High Court in Maharastra, India. He acquired his Bachelor’s Degree in Law from Shivaji University in India followed by Master’s Degree in International Business from IESEG School of Management in Paris, France.
