How?

Combine first-class professional experience and cultural immersion. This is our flagship program. If you’re looking to combine a career-accelerating internship with the travel adventure of a lifetime, look no further.

1. Apply

Fill out our short application form and submit your resume/CV on our Apply Now page. We will contact you within 48 Hours & schedule a meeting with you according to your convenience in the same week.

2. Interview with our Admissions Team

We will discuss the opportunities available within your desired program destination and career fields. Come prepared to talk about your previous professional experience, goals and motivations. This is your chance to shine and show us why you would be a good candidate for our program.

3. Confirm your spot with a deposit

After your interview, you will be informed within a week if you have been accepted into the program. After receiving your acceptance letter you will have two weeks to pay 50% of the program fee to ensure that you’ve confirmed a spot on the program. Please note that in the unlikely event there's a cancellation of your program from our side and we are unable to continue with your program or in case of your visa rejection, we will give you a full refund.

Once You're On-Board With Us This Will Be The Following Process

1. Online Meeting

We want to ensure that we find you the best internship match. You will then have an online meeting on Google Meet with the host organization to ensure that it is the best match on both ends.

2. Visa assistance

We know that traveling to a new country can be difficult to plan logistically, which is why our team of visa experts are here to help you arrive on program hassle-free.

3. Pack your bags and travel

Once you’ve confirmed your role and start date, you’ll be able to purchase your plane tickets. We’ll send you helpful pre-departure materials via email, including a welcome pack about your new city, so that you’re fully prepared.

4. Airport pick-up

We want you to arrive with zero worries, which is why we will pick you up at the airport and bring you to your accommodation.

5. Welcome orientation

We’ll organize an orientation for you and other program participants shortly after arrival, so that you can meet the team, make new friends, and learn about your new city.

6. Internship

Realize your potential by interning in your field in an important global city.

Training & Internship

On the first day of your internship you'll have your training session so that you are aware of your role & your tasks/ responsibilities. Your Internship will be from the second day till the end of your program.

Social Events

We want you to feel immersed in your new city, which is why the program includes weekly Social Events.

7. Exit interview

When your internship finishes you’ll have a one on one exit interview with our team to talk about your experience. You’ll also receive an Internship completion certificate on your program complition.

8. Apply for your dream job

Our Training & Internship program will leverage your Resume with International Internship Experience which will be very useful in your future Interviews.

About Us

At AmbiSeek, we inspire and encourage students to seek their ambition, believe in and challenge themselves and realize their potential. We are dedicated to bring this purpose to life for every single program participant. We are driven by our purpose and unified by our values.
